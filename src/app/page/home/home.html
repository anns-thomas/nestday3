Kerala Government: A Model of Progressive Governance

The Kerala Government is the administrative authority of the Indian state of Kerala. It operates through three branches: the executive, legislature, and judiciary. The executive branch is headed by the Chief Minister and the Governor, while the legislative branch consists of the Kerala Legislative Assembly. The government is known for its strong focus on education, healthcare, social welfare, and decentralized local governance. Kerala has achieved high literacy rates and human development indicators compared to many other Indian states.
